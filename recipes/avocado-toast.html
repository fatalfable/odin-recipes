Avocado toast

The best avocado toast ever!

This 4-ingredient egg avocado toast, topped with chopped hard boiled egg and sprinkled with everything bagel seasoning, makes a satisfying breakfast or lunch in just minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 slice of bread
  • 1/2 cup mashed avocado
  • 1 boiled egg
  • 1 teaspoon everything bagel seasoning

Steps

  1. Smear mashed avocado on toast.
  2. Sprinkle with everything bagel seasoning.
  3. Top with egg.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
